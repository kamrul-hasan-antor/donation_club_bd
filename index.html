Donation plan

Our main goal is to stand by the flood affected people by doing
the following tasks.

Food for everyone

We are providing food items for everyone. For example, rice, pulses, pure water, orceline and some dry food. Due to floods, there is a shortage of clean water. So we are providing clean water.

Shelter for all

Many people have already become homeless due to the floods. So we are moving them to certain places. As shelters are less, many are helping to take shelter in high-rise buildings.

Medicine for everyone

Due to lack of food and clean water, people get affected by various water borne diseases. Also various diseases occur including cold, fever, diarrhea. So we providing treatement to all.

Their lives depend on your donation

In the last few weeks, millions of people in the Sylhet and Sunamganj districts have experienced heavy rains and flash floods, leaving them homeless and without possessions. With homes crushed, objects floating away and people missing, the country is at high alert

More than 4.3 million people have been affected by floods and waterlogging. Millions have been leave home and also face the challenge of electricity. So people are unable to keep in contact with family and friends.
